# Laboratory Work №4: Behavioral Diagrams

## Overview
Folder contains Behavioral Diagrams. These diagrams illustrate the dynamic behavior of systems, showing how objects interact over time and the flow of activities within specific processes.

## Contents
The following diagrams:

1. **ATM System Sequence Diagram (`atm sys.jpg`)**
   - **Type:** Sequence Diagram.
   - **Description:** Showss the step-by-step interaction between the User, ATM machine, Bank system and Database during a cash withdrawal process (PIN verification, balance check, and transaction approval).

2. **Medical Clinic Activity Diagram (`med sys.jpg`)**
   - **Type:** Activity Diagram.
   - **Description:** Shows the workflow of a patient's visit to a clinic, from registration and consultation to lab testing, treatment and final discharge or hospital admission.

3. **Car Insurance Activity Diagram (`car ins.jpg`)**
   - **Type:** Activity Diagram.
   - **Description:** Shows the process of filing an insurance claim, including policy verification, damage assessment, document requests and the final decision to approve or reject the payment.

## Objective
The goal of this lab was to:
- Understand the dynamic aspects of system design.
- Model the time-ordered sequence of interactions using Sequence Diagrams.
- Map complex business processes and decision logic using Activity Diagrams.
